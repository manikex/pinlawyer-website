We are pleased to announce that **Pin Lawyer** has expanded its litigation practice to the **Telangana High Court** and the **Andhra Pradesh High Court**.

This expansion ensures that clients across the Telangana and Andhra Pradesh regions can now access affordable, trusted legal representation without travelling to Delhi or other metros.

## What This Means for Clients

- **Local representation** — our advocates now appear before both High Courts regularly.
- **Remote case management** — clients continue to receive updates via WhatsApp and email.
- **Affordable fees** — the same transparent, economical fee structures apply.

## Practice Areas Covered

We accept briefs in civil, criminal, constitutional, and service matters before both High Courts. Our team is fully equipped to handle writ petitions, appeals, and original suits.

If you are based in Telangana or Andhra Pradesh and need legal assistance, visit our **Pin Appointment** page or contact us directly.
